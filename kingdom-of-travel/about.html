About KingdomOfTravel

KingdomOfTravel is Canada's independent guide to travel rewards — built for Canadians who want to travel better without spending more.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves to experience premium travel. Business class seats and luxury hotel rooms aren't only for the wealthy — they're accessible to anyone willing to learn how credit card points, airline miles, and hotel rewards actually work.

Since 2019, we've helped over 85,000 Canadians earn their first business class seat, book dream honeymoons on points, and understand that a $799 annual fee credit card can be the best financial decision they ever make.

Editorial Standards

We only recommend cards, airlines, and hotels we have personally used or thoroughly researched. Our rankings are based on objective criteria — welcome bonus value, earn rates, and redemption flexibility — not on who pays us the most referral revenue.

We earn referral commissions when readers apply for credit cards through our links. This is how we keep the site free. It never changes our rankings: cards that don't meet our standards simply don't appear on our lists.

The Team

KingdomOfTravel is written by a small team of Canadian travel enthusiasts who collectively hold over 40 credit cards, have flown in more than 25 business class products, and have stayed in some of the world's most extraordinary hotels — the vast majority on points.
