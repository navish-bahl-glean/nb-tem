How to get there using miles & points

Asia-Pacific

Japan — Tokyo, Osaka, Kyoto

Japan is one of the best business class sweet spots on the planet. ANA's The Room and JAL's Sky Suite are world-class products bookable with Aeroplan points from as few as 55,000 one-way from Vancouver.

  • ANA Business: from 55,000 Aeroplan pts (YVR–NRT)
  • JAL Business: from 60,000 Aeroplan pts
  • Best booking window: 355 days out

Europe

Europe — London, Paris, Rome, Barcelona

Business class to Europe is attainable from 60,000 points one-way. Lufthansa Group Business (Swiss, Austrian, Brussels Airlines) offers lie-flat seats at rates many overlook.

  • Air Canada Business: from 65,000 Aeroplan pts
  • Lufthansa Business: from 58,000 Aeroplan pts
  • British Airways Club World via Avios: from 62,500 pts

Indian Ocean

Maldives — Overwater Bungalows on Points

The Maldives is one of the world's most sought-after hotel points destinations. Conrad Maldives Rangali Island and Waldorf Astoria Maldives regularly feature on "best redemptions" lists — and for good reason.

  • Conrad Maldives: from 95,000 Hilton pts/night
  • Waldorf Astoria: from 120,000 Hilton pts/night
  • Park Hyatt: from 30,000 World of Hyatt pts/night

Pacific

Australia & New Zealand — Long-Haul Done Right

Business class to Sydney or Auckland is an incredible product — think Qantas Business Suite or Air New Zealand Business Premier. These 16+ hour flights are exactly what premium redemptions are made for.

  • Qantas Business: from 80,000 Aeroplan pts (YVR–SYD)
  • Air NZ Business Premier: from 75,000 Aeroplan pts
  • Best routing: one-stop via LAX or HNL
